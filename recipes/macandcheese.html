Mac and Cheese

Description:

It's mac and cheese. You know what it is. It's pasta with a cheese sauce. You know what it tastes like unless you've been living under a rock forever

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Shredded Cheese Blend
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil water and add pasta, allow to cook until al dente
  2. Drain pasta water, save some in a small bowl and set aside alongside the pasta
  3. In a seperate bowl, whisk your flour, onion powder, salt, and pepper into a mix
  4. In your original pot, melt butter over medium heat, and slowly add in your flour mix
  5. Slowly add in your milk, whisking continuously until smooth
  6. Lower heat and add in your shredded cheeses until all melted
  7. Add your macaroni back into the pot and stir until fully coated
